My Top Seven Tips on Getting Started to Becoming A Web Developer

  1. Start learning basic HTML5, CSS3, JS, JSON and other web language concepts.
  2. Create a small scale project whether enrolled in a course such as CS601 or in any accredited institution or on your own.
  3. Collaborate with others on your ideas for small or large web development projects.
  4. Review technical concepts and keep practicing on coding before applying for jobs.
  5. Once you reviewed all that is needed, focus on entry level web programming positions at reasonable salary and either nearby your location or remote.
  6. After applying for entry level jobs, acing the interviews, and getting the job, work really hard to maintain what you learned before and after interview to keep succeeding!
